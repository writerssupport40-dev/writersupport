// FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before submitting a request.

How long does turnaround take? ▾
Standard turnaround is 3–7 business days depending on scope. Rush delivery (24–48h) is available at an additional fee. Genomic analysis projects may take 1–2 weeks.
Is my research kept confidential? ▾
Absolutely. All submissions are treated with strict confidentiality. We can sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) upon request before any files are shared.
How do I share my files with you? ▾
After your request is confirmed, we'll provide a secure Google Drive link or email address for file submission. We accept Word docs, Excel sheets, raw data files, and more.
Do you offer revisions? ▾
Yes. All services include at least one round of revisions at no extra cost. Additional revision rounds can be arranged if needed.
How is payment handled? ▾
After we agree on scope and price, we'll send you a PayPal invoice. Payment is typically split — 50% upfront, 50% on delivery — for larger projects.
Can I request a consultation before committing? ▾
Yes — and we encourage it. Select "Book a Consultation" in the form and we'll schedule a free 20-minute call via Google Calendar to discuss your project.
