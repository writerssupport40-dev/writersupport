Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our professional services and how we work with researchers.

How long does editing take?
Standard turnaround is 5-7 business days. Rush services (24-48 hours) are available for an additional fee.

Absolutely. We sign NDAs upon request and use secure, encrypted channels for all file transfers. Your research is safe with us.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes, all services include up to two rounds of revisions to ensure the final output meets your exact requirements.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. For institutional clients, we also support purchase orders.
