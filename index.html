Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical turnaround time? ▼
Most projects are completed within 5-7 business days. Rush services are available for an additional fee. We'll confirm your specific timeline during the consultation.
Do you offer discounts for bulk services? ▼
Yes! We offer special pricing for multiple manuscripts, datasets, or ongoing partnerships. Contact us for a customized quote.
Is my research kept confidential? ▼
Absolutely. We maintain strict confidentiality agreements. Your data and research are never shared with third parties and are securely stored.
Can you help with manuscripts in other languages? ▼
We specialize in English-language manuscripts. For other languages, we can refer you to trusted partner services.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and digital payment platforms. Payment is due upon project confirmation.
Do you provide revisions? ▼
Yes! All our services include one round of revisions at no additional cost. Additional revision rounds are available for a small fee.
