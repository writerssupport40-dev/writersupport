1. Introduction

At Writers Support ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and when using our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number through service request forms
  • Project details, manuscript files, research data, and other documents you submit
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Communication preferences and feedback

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system, device identifiers)
  • IP address and approximate location (city/region level)
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and click patterns
  • Referral source and exit pages

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and delivering our services (editing, analysis, consulting, etc.)
  • Processing service requests and managing client relationships
  • Sending confirmations, invoices, and receipt notifications
  • Responding to inquiries and providing customer support
  • Improving our website functionality and user experience
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our terms

4. Data Security & Confidentiality

Your privacy and data security are paramount to us. We implement the following measures:

  • Encrypted Communication: SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted to and from our servers
  • Secure Storage: Research data, manuscripts, and sensitive files are stored on secured, password-protected systems
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel with a legitimate need-to-know have access to client data
  • Non-Disclosure: All team members sign strict confidentiality agreements
  • NDA Policy: Upon request, we provide comprehensive Non-Disclosure Agreements for added protection

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, email delivery systems, and hosting providers
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or regulatory authority
  • Website Analytics: We use tools like Google Analytics to understand usage patterns

6. Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Access & Portability

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you in a portable format.

6.2 Correction & Deletion

You may request that we correct inaccurate information or delete your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

6.3 Opt-Out of Marketing

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking "Unsubscribe" in the email footer.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Client Data: Retained for 6 months to 2 years after project completion
  • Communication Records: Retained for up to 3 years
  • Financial Records: Retained per accounting and tax regulations (typically 7 years)

8. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience. These include:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices (language, theme preferences)

12. Contact Us

For questions, requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices:

  • Email: hello@writerssupport.com
  • Response Time: We respond to all requests within 30 days
